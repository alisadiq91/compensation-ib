Privacy Policy

At Compensation Kings, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our clients' personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect
We collect personal information that is necessary to provide our claims compensation services to you. This information may include your name, address, phone number, email address, date of birth, and any other information required to evaluate and process your claim.

We may also collect information about your use of our website, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and pages visited. This information is used to improve our website and user experience.

How We Use Your Information
We use your personal information to provide you with our claims compensation services, including processing your claim and communicating with you about its status. We may also use your information to contact you with marketing messages about our services that we believe may be of interest to you. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share your information with our service providers and partners as necessary to provide our services to you.

Data Security
We have implemented reasonable and appropriate physical, technical, and administrative measures to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. We regularly review and update our security measures to ensure they remain effective.

Your Rights
You have the right to request access to and correction of your personal information that we hold. You may also have the right to request the erasure of your personal information, restrict its processing, or object to its processing. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at info@compensation.co.uk
